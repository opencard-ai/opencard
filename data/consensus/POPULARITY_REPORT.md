### Consensus Takeaways

- **Chase dominates** the travel space with 5+ consistently recommended cards
- **Amex** leads premium with Platinum/Gold but business cards also strong
- **Capital One** emerging as a top 3 issuer with Venture family
- **No single "best" card** - optimal setup depends on spending patterns
- **Annual fees are climbing** but credits/benefits offset for heavy users
- **Bilt** is the notable 2025-2026 newcomer (rent payments as category)
