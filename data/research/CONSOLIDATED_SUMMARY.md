## Key Findings & Market Trends (2025-2026)
1. **Premium Card Rebranding**: Bank of America and Alaska Airlines successfully transitioned the Alaska Visa to the **Atmos Rewards** ecosystem. The **Atmos Summit ($395)** has emerged as a major competitor in the premium airline card market.
2. **Annual Fee Reductions**: **U.S. Bank Altitude Connect** has solidified its position as a top-tier "no annual fee" travel card after dropping its $95 fee in mid-2024.
3. **Niche Category Focus**: **Wells Fargo Attune** has introduced a unique 4% cash back model for "self-care" and "sustainability," marking a shift towards lifestyle-based rewards rather than traditional travel/dining.
4. **Citi's Premium Return**: The **Citi Strata Elite ($595)** has finally replaced the long-vacant slot in Citi's premium lineup, offering unique American Airlines lounge access (passes) which was previously rare for Citi proprietary cards.
