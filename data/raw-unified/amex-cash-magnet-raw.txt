back on Amazon.com.</li></ol><h2><span id="Disadvantages">Disadvantages</span></h2><ol><li>It has foreign transaction fee, so it's not a good choice outside the US.</li><li>This credit card is on the points with <a href="https://www.uscreditcardguide.com/amex-everyday-credit-card/" target="_blank">AmEx ED</a>, <a href="https://www.uscreditcardguide.com/amex-everyday-preferred-credit-card/" You will not lose the MR points on one card if you close the account, but you will lose all of the MR points if you close all of the MR cards. Specifically, having held Cash Magnet makes you ineligible for the welcome offer of BCE. So if you want both, you should apply for BCE first.</li><li>AmEx doesn&#8217;t care about the number of hard pulls.</li><li>You can try to apply for it when you have a credit history of 3 months.</li><li>You must wait at least 90 days between the application for ED and EDP, otherwise the application will be cancelled.</li><li><del>Only one AmEx credit card can be approved in a day. This rule does not apply to charge cards, you can be approved for one credit card and multiple charge cards in the day.</del> Multiple cards approved in the same day will only have one Hard Pull.</li><li>Only 2 AmEx credit cards can be approved within 90 days, but again, this rule does not apply to charge cards.</li><li>You can keep at most 5 AmEx credit cards, not including charge cards.</li></ol><h2><span id="Summary">Summary</span></h2><p>The card is said to compete with <a href="https://www.uscreditcardguide.com/chase-freedom-unlimited-credit-card/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Chase Freedom Unlimited</a>, but CFU does have advantage if you hold any Chase premium card that can boost the points you earned on CFU card.
